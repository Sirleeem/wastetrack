System documentation Mandate, scope, and institutional context

Official description

Design and Implementation of a Waste Management Reporting and Collection Optimization System

A final-year project of the Department of Computer Science, Abubakar Tafawa Balewa University, Bauchi.

Functions

  • Residents submit geolocated waste reports with optional photographic evidence
  • Administrators verify, assign, schedule and monitor cases
  • Collection officers update field task progress through to completion
  • A lightweight optimization module recommends collection order by proximity and urgency

Scope boundaries

Physical IoT bin sensors, payment processing, government identity integration, and industrial multi-vehicle fleet logistics are outside the scope of this undergraduate prototype.
