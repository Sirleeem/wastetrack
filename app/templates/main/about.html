About this project

Design and Implementation of a Waste Management Reporting and Collection Optimization System

Prepared as a final-year project proposal for the Department of Computer Science, Abubakar Tafawa Balewa University Bauchi.

What the system does

  • Residents submit geolocated waste reports with optional images
  • Administrators verify, assign, schedule and track cases
  • Collection officers update task progress in the field
  • Optimization recommends an efficient pickup order using proximity and urgency

Out of scope

Physical IoT sensors, payment processing, government identity integration and full industrial fleet logistics are intentionally excluded for this undergraduate prototype.
