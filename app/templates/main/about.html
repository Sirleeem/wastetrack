About WasteTrack Bauchi

Local refuse reporting and collection coordination prototype for Bauchi State, Nigeria.

Project title

Design and Implementation of a Waste Management Reporting and Collection Optimization System

Final-year project · Department of Computer Science, Abubakar Tafawa Balewa University (ATBU), Bauchi, Nigeria.

The system is framed for Bauchi community use, in the same operational setting as local sanitation work led by bodies such as the Bauchi State Environmental Protection Agency (BASEPA): residents report refuse with map locations; administrators verify and schedule; collection officers complete field tasks. Sample coordinates centre on Bauchi metropolis.

What it supports locally

  • Overflowing public bins and scattered refuse around markets and motor parks
  • Illegal dumping on open plots and roadside
  • Blocked drains that worsen during the rainy season
  • Missed household or commercial collection points
  • Basic route ordering for nearby pending stops

Scope (prototype)

IoT bin sensors, payments, national ID login, and full city fleet GPS tracking are outside this undergraduate prototype. The focus is a practical web system for Bauchi-style reporting and collection coordination.
