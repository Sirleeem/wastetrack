About WasteTrack Bauchi

Refuse reporting and collection coordination for Bauchi State, Nigeria.

The system

Design and Implementation of a Waste Management Reporting and Collection Optimization System

WasteTrack is a web portal for Bauchi residents, collection officers, and administrators. Residents report refuse with map locations; administrators verify and schedule; officers complete field tasks. Department of Computer Science, Abubakar Tafawa Balewa University (ATBU), Bauchi.

What it supports

  • Overflowing public bins and scattered refuse around markets and motor parks
  • Illegal dumping on open plots and roadside
  • Blocked drains that worsen during the rainy season
  • Missed household or commercial collection points
  • Route ordering for nearby pending stops

Access

Residents can register online. Administrators and collection officers sign in with authorised accounts issued by system operators.
