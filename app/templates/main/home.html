How it works

From roadside dumps to cleared collection points

Clear locations and tracked cases help crews respond faster when refuse piles up on streets and at neighbourhood collection centres.

01 - Residents

Report the dump you see

Pin overflow and illegal dumping along walls, markets, and estate roads. Add a short description and optional photo so the authority can verify the site.

02 - Collection officers

Clear assigned stops on the ground

Officers open mapped tasks, work drains and collection points, and mark completion so residents can see that the case has moved.

03 - Authority desk

Coordinate verification and scheduling

Administrators review the queue, set urgency, assign officers, and group nearby reports for a practical evacuation order across Bauchi neighbourhoods.

Our purpose

A practical channel for cleaner streets

WasteTrack connects residents who see refuse problems with the teams that clear them. Every case is recorded with location, status, and history from report to completion.

  • Report overflow, illegal dumping, and blocked drains
  • Track status without repeated phone follow-ups
  • Help officers plan nearby stops more efficiently
  • Give administrators a single view of open cases
