Local waste operations

From collection centres to street evacuations

In Bauchi metropolis, solid waste is often moved from neighbourhood collection points by agency trucks and field teams. This portal digitises the reporting and task side of that work so cases are easier to verify, assign, and close.

01 - Residents

Report the dump or overflow you see

Pin places such as markets, motor parks, estates, and roadside collection points. Add a short note and optional photo so officers know what to expect on arrival.

02 - Collection officers

Work from assigned stops

Field staff open mapped tasks, start collection on site, and mark completion - similar to how solid waste evacuation exercises move from one collection centre to another across town.

03 - Authority desk

Coordinate verification and scheduling

Administrators review the queue, set urgency, assign officers, and group nearby reports for a practical pickup order across Bauchi roads and neighbourhoods.

About BASEPA context

Built for Bauchi sanitation realities

The Bauchi State Environmental Protection Agency (BASEPA) is the state body responsible for environmental protection and solid waste evacuation across Bauchi. WasteTrack is an academic software prototype that models digital reporting and collection coordination for that local setting.

  • Report overflow, illegal dumping, and blocked drains
  • Track status without repeated phone follow-ups
  • Help officers plan nearby stops more efficiently
  • Give administrators a single view of open cases
