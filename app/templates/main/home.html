Institutional purpose

A structured channel between the community and waste authorities

Informal complaints by phone or word of mouth make it difficult to verify locations, prioritise cases, or measure response. This system captures waste issues as structured records—location, category, urgency, and status—so collection can be planned with clearer operational visibility.

  • Central register of reported waste incidents
  • Role-based review, assignment, and completion
  • Route order support using proximity and priority
  • Full status history for each case

System capabilities

Operational features of the portal

Map-based reporting

Incidents are pinned on OpenStreetMap with coordinates stored for dispatch and planning.

Case status history

Every transition—from submission to completion—is time-stamped for audit and follow-up.

Role separation

Residents, collection officers, and administrators work in separate, permissioned workspaces.

Collection sequencing

Pending stops can be ordered by proximity and urgency to support field routing decisions.
