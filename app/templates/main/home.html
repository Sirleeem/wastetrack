Why this portal

Built for the street, trusted for operations

Patterns used by leading waste authorities and commercial operators: clear reporting, local dispatch, and measurable response.

01 — Reliable

Every report is a tracked case

Submissions capture category, description, location, and optional photo evidence — so nothing depends on a lost phone call or informal complaint.

02 — Local

Field officers get clear stops

{# Mission — LAWMA institutional narrative #}

Our mandate

A structured channel between the community and waste authorities

Informal complaints by phone or word of mouth make it hard to verify locations, prioritise cases, or measure response. WasteTrack captures waste issues as structured records so collection can be planned with clearer operational visibility — in the spirit of professional waste authorities and modern collection partners.

  • Central register of reported waste incidents
  • Verification, assignment, and scheduling workflows
  • Proximity-based collection order support
  • Status history for service accountability
