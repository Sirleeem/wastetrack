{# Why local #}

Why Bauchi needs this

From market gate to Yelwa road — one clear channel

Many refuse complaints still move by phone call or word of mouth. That makes it hard to confirm the place, prioritise urgent sites, or know when a team has finished the job.

01 — Residents

Report where the problem is

Drop a pin near the market, mosque, motor park, or estate road. Add a short description and optional photo so the authority can see the real situation.

02 — Collection team

Clear assigned stops on the ground

Officers see mapped tasks for Bauchi areas, start work on site, and mark completion so residents can follow the status.

03 — Authority

Plan collection with better visibility

Administrators verify cases, set urgency, assign officers, and group nearby reports for a practical collection order across the LGA.

{# Mandate #}

Our purpose in Bauchi

A practical tool for cleaner streets and better response

WasteTrack helps close the gap between people who see refuse problems and the teams responsible for clearing them — with records you can follow from submission to completion.

  • Report overflow, illegal dumping, and blocked drains
  • Track status without repeated phone follow-ups
  • Help officers plan nearby stops more efficiently
  • Give administrators a single view of open cases
